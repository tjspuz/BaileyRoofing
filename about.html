Our Roofing Services

Bailey Roofing Contractors is a locally-owned business that was founded in the year 1989. We've been serving the Iowa and Midwest area with superior roofing services for commercial properties.

Whether you want a roof installed for your new property or you need roof maintenance services, you can rely on our union journeymen roofers for quality roofing services.

We have received many awards for our services including the Firestone President's Club. Our success is due to our dedication to our service and the ethics we follow. We've built relations with some of the leading property owners in the area.

We're fully licensed, insured, and bonded and we have a manufacturer's certificate. Rely on us for our 24/7 emergency services. Call us for FREE estimates today!
